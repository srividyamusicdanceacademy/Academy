Meet our founder and primary educator

Smt. Vid. Bindu Raj N

Founder, Director & Government Examiner

Smt. Vid. Bindu Raj N is an accomplished dance and music educator with over a decade of rich pedagogical experience spanning classical arts and higher academic commerce instruction. As an officially appointed Government Bharatanatyam Examiner, she brings unparalleled academic rigor and standardized excellence directly to the classroom.

Her teaching philosophy seamlessly balances technical mastery with holistic development, encouraging pupils to find their expressive voice while respecting centuries-old classical traditions.

Vidwat in Bharatanatyam KSEEB Board Certified
Junior Cert. in Carnatic Music KSEEB Board Certified
Prestigious Titles "Nrithya Milinda" & "Kannada Natya Rani"
